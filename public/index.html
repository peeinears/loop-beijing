LOOP is user generated radio for Beijing buses. Enter the app + listen to custom soundtracks for bus lines from JUE venues. Upload a message for fellow JUE attendees, get the nearest JUE venue to your current location. LOOP is an audio tour of Beijing + your city guide to JUE.
